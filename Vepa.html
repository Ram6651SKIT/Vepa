HISTORY OF THE COMPANY

  • Vepa & Co., a firm of Chartered Accountants, was set up in the year 1972 in Chennai by Mr. Vepa Krishna to provide professional services in auditing and taxation services.

  • Over the last 4 decades, the firm has built its reputation by providing independent professional advice to several Companies, Partnership Firms, Proprietary Concerns, Societies, Associations and Trusts with uncompromising professional standards and ethics.

  • We are today a 4 member firm, with our head office at Chennai and a branch at Bangalore. Our service offerings include Audit & Assurance, Risk Advisory, Tax Advisory, Corporate Advisory and Outsourcing services.

  • We are confident to provide our domestic and international clients with creative solutions to deal effectively with accounting, financial, legal and regulatory challenges.

  • We are committed to providing quality professional assistance and aim to secure client satisfaction.
